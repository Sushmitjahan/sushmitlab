Welcome to Sushmit Computational Neuroscience Lab

Under the leadership of Sushmit Jahan, our lab pioneers research in computational neuroscience, focusing on EEG signal processing and developing novel neurotechnologies for mental health diagnostics and treatment.

Our interdisciplinary approach combines expertise in computer science, neuroscience, and artificial intelligence to create innovative solutions for neurological conditions such as depression using advanced computational methods.

Our Mission

Our mission is to computationally explore brain complexity and plasticity using EEG and neurotechnology, develop advanced BCI solutions, and transform mental health diagnostics—making depression treatment more accessible worldwide.

Lab Location

Center for Interdisciplinary Research

Research Focus

Innovative projects at the cutting edge of computational neuroscience

EEG-based Depression Diagnosis

Developing computational methods to diagnose major depressive disorder using electroencephalogram (EEG) data analysis and machine learning algorithms.

Self Funding

Neuroplasticity & Treatment

Assessing the effects of NN-Dimethyltryptamine and Aripiprazole on brain plasticity in treatment-resistant depression using computational neurotechniques.

Funded by CIR, Varendra University

AI for Emotion Detection

Developing artificial intelligence models to detect depression and other mental health conditions from human emotional patterns.

Research Equipment

State-of-the-art technology enabling our neuroscience research

Emotiv EPOC X 14-Channel EEG Headset

Our lab is equipped with the state-of-the-art Emotiv EPOC X 14-channel EEG headset, enabling high-quality brain signal acquisition for our research in computational neuroscience.

This advanced neurotechnology allows us to capture and analyze brainwave patterns with exceptional precision, facilitating our investigations into depression diagnosis and neural plasticity.

14 saline-based electrodes
High sampling rate
Wireless connectivity
Real-time processing
Motion sensors
Research-grade software
